Then, large drilling rigs and explosives are used to break the gypsum into smaller fragments, which are then loaded onto trucks for transportation to the processing plant.</p><p>In underground mining, shafts or tunnels are excavated to access the gypsum deposit. The dissolved gypsum is then pumped to the surface for further processing.</p><p>Once the gypsum has been extracted from the mine, it undergoes several processing steps before it can be used for its intended applications.
